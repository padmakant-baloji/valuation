## Blog Structure Template

Each blog post should include:

1. **SEO-Optimized Title** (60 characters max)
2. **Meta Description** (150-160 characters)
3. **H1 with Primary Keyword**
4. **Introduction** (150-200 words)
5. **H2 Sections** with keyword variations
6. **H3 Subsections** for detailed content
7. **FAQ Section** (3-5 questions)
8. **Internal Links** to service pages and homepage
9. **CTA Section** (Request Valuation)
10. **Schema Markup** (Article schema, FAQ schema)

## Internal Linking Strategy

- Link from blogs to relevant service pages
- Link from service pages to location pages
- Link from homepage to all service pages
- Use descriptive anchor text with keywords
- Maintain 3-5 internal links per blog post
