## How to Download Remaining Logos

1. **Visit Official Websites**: Check each company's official website for logo downloads in their "About Us", "Media Kit", or "Brand Guidelines" sections

2. **Use Logo Databases**:
   - CompaniesLogo.com - Search for each company name
   - Wikimedia Commons - For publicly available logos
   - Google Images - Search "[Company Name] logo PNG transparent"

3. **Save Requirements**:
   - Format: PNG with transparent background (preferred) or JPG
   - Size: Minimum 200x80px, recommended 400x160px
   - File naming: Must match exactly (e.g., `chola-finance.png`, `nivara-home-fin.png`)

4. **Place Files**: Save all logo files in `/public/clients/` directory

Once all logos are added, the ClientsSection component will automatically display them on your website!
